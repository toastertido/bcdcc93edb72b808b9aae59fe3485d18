Netflix

Minecraft: Story Mode is no longer on Netflix. Please do not ask how to play it on Netflix, please use the Netflix Archive YouTube channel.

The Netflix Archive Episodes 1-5 

The Netflix Archive is a YouTube channel that's main goal is to archive the version of the game that was on Netflix until it got taken down on December 4th, 2022. Fortunately, AsteroidBK and SrBig archived the videos and have since been adapted to YouTube as a very basic point and click adventure using the end screen function on YouTube, although SrBig's videos were not used because Asteroid had already archived and made them public before he had. The trailers and YouTube playable episodes were made and archived by Pox1016. Along with the game itself, on the YouTube channel has also archived many things such as media related to the Netflix version and other language dubs. So far, episodes 1 - 5 have been ported, but only as male Jesse as of now. Female Jesse is in development right now, and you can view progress on the Netflix Archive Discord server.

Block By Block - The Amulet Recreation

Wazzy is creating a super polished rewrite of Story Mode, with new character designs, animation style, updated graphics, features, and more. This will be a series on YouTube coming out in 2025. As of now, a trailer is out showing what this recreation has to offer. This genuinely looks amazing, you should check this out.

Season Two Interactive Episodes 1-5 (S2)

Riley has taken it upon himself to port Season Two to YouTube as well! So far, five out of five episodes are done for the Male Variant (with some compromises, to be fixed in future), with the Female Variant in Development. You can find his channel here. Please note that it is currently incomplete. It does not save your choices across episodes. You can view progress in the same Discord server. The project is said not to be continued until 2025.

MCSM: World's Edge Fan-Fic Sequel

This is a fan continuation of the series that involves new deities and even a multiverse with an overarching plot. Recently it has had voice acting and a "spider-verse" type plot. Animated in Mineimator by KenTracer.
